Pizza con Patatine Fritte

Description:

Pizza con Patatine Fritte is a fun and indulgent twist on classic Italian pizza, combining the best of both worlds‚Äîcrispy French fries and cheesy, saucy goodness. It starts with a thin, golden crust topped with rich tomato sauce and creamy mozzarella, baked until bubbly and fragrant.

Once out of the oven, a generous handful of freshly fried, crispy fries is scattered on top, adding a satisfying crunch and a salty kick. Some variations drizzle ketchup or mayonnaise for extra indulgence. This playful and comforting pizza is a favorite among those who love bold flavors and a bit of crunch in every bite.

Ingredients:

  • For the Dough:

    • 250g all-purpose flour or pizza flour
    • 150ml warm water
    • 1 teaspoon salt

    • 1 teaspoon dry yeast
    • 1 tablespoon olive oil

  • For the Toppings:

    • 150g mozzarella cheese (shredded or sliced)
    • 150ml tomato sauce (made from crushed tomatoes, olive oil, salt, and oregano)
    • 150g French fries (freshly fried or oven-baked)

    • Optional: Ketchup, mayonnaise, or truffle oil for extra flavor

Steps:

  1. Prepare the Dough: Mix flour, warm water, yeast, sugar, and salt in a bowl. Add olive oil and knead until smooth. Let it rise for 1-2 hours until doubled in size.
  2. Preheat the Oven: Set your oven to 220¬∞C (430¬∞F) so it's ready for baking.
  3. Shape the Dough: Roll out the dough into a circle and place it on a baking tray.
  4. Add Sauce and Cheese: Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough and sprinkle mozzarella on top.
  5. Bakethe Pizza: Put the pizza in the oven and bake for 10-12 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  6. Cook the Fries: While the pizza is baking, fry or bake the French fries until crispy.
  7. Assemble the Pizza: Once the pizza is out of the oven, top it with the crispy fries. Add salt or drizzle ketchup/mayo if desired.
